# Amazon Category-Specific Rules Reference

## Electronics
- **Gated:** Some sub-categories (streaming media players, car electronics)
- FCC certification required for wireless/radio frequency devices
- UL or equivalent safety listing for electrical items
- Accurate technical specs (model number, compatibility, wattage, frequency)
- No altered/removed serial numbers
- Cannot circumvent copyright protection or descramble signals
- Battery safety: lithium battery products require hazmat classification and SDS
- Title format: Brand + Model + Product Type + Key Spec + Compatibility
- Character limit: 150 chars recommended
- Invoices must be from authorized distributors for brand gating approval

## Clothing, Shoes & Jewelry / Apparel
- **Gated:** Luxury jewelry; most apparel ungated for basic items
- Required attributes: Department (Men/Women/Boys/Girls/Baby), Size, Color, Material, Style
- Size charts strongly recommended (reduce returns)
- Licensed sports apparel (Nike, Adidas, Under Armour) → must list in Sports, not Clothing
- Color naming: use Amazon standard color names (see Valid Values tab)
- Title format: Brand + Department + Product Type + Style/Feature + Size Range
- Title character limit: 80–150 chars (stricter than general)
- FBA: apparel-specific fulfillment fees apply
- Apparel images: mannequin or model preferred for main image (white background still required)
- Multiple angle shots important (front, back, detail, size chart)

## Health & Personal Care / Beauty
- **Gated:** Requires approval; invoices from authorized distributors needed
- FDA compliance: no unsubstantiated drug claims
- Ingredient list: complete, accurate, INCI names for cosmetics
- MoCRA (Modernization of Cosmetics Regulation Act) compliance required Dec 2025:
  - Serious adverse events reported within 15 days
  - Facility registration with FDA
  - Safety substantiation records
- No claims: "cures," "treats," "prevents" any disease (FDA drug claim territory)
- Allowed: "moisturizes," "soothes," "supports healthy..."
- Supplements: additional requirements — see dietary supplement guidelines
- Hazmat: products with flammable ingredients need SDS

## Grocery & Gourmet Food
- **Gated:** Requires approval
- FDA labeling compliance: Nutrition Facts panel, ingredient list, allergen declarations
- Shelf-life management: FBA requires minimum remaining shelf life at time of receipt
- Expiration dates must be on packaging and in the listing
- Organic claims: USDA Organic certification required
- No health claims without substantiation
- Temperature-sensitive items: cold chain documentation

## Toys & Games
- **Mostly ungated** for general toys
- **Gated sub-categories:** Certain high-value items, specific brands
- Children's Product Certificate (CPC) required for children's toys (ages 0–12)
- ASTM F963 toy safety standards compliance
- Age-grading: must match product packaging
- Choking hazard warning mandatory if applicable (small parts, small balls)
- Magnetic toys: ASTM F963 magnetic flux index compliance
- Electronic toys: electrical safety testing + battery safety documentation
- Seasonal note: Oct–Nov toy listings get heavy scrutiny pre-holidays

## Baby Products
- CPC required for direct-contact products (bedding, clothing, feeding)
- ASTM and CPSC standards apply
- No drop-side cribs (banned)
- Car seats: specific safety certifications
- No claims about sleep safety unless clinically substantiated
- Suffocation/strangulation hazards: mandatory warnings

## Sports & Outdoors
- **Ungated** for most products
- Sports apparel from major athletic brands must be in Sports, not Clothing
- Licensed sports merchandise: must include league name, team name, athlete name
- Sports apparel image requirement: stricter than general clothing
- Firearms accessories: no listing for magazine converters, silencers, illegal mods
- Hunting: state/federal compliance required for certain items

## Home & Kitchen
- **Ungated** — one of the most accessible categories
- Chemical products: SDS may be required (cleaning products, adhesives)
- Electrical appliances: UL/ETL certification recommended
- Candles: burn time, material (soy/paraffin/beeswax), wick type
- Food contact items: FDA compliance, BPA-free claims need substantiation

## Automotive
- **Gated:** Requires approval
- Fitment data critical: Year, Make, Model, Trim, Engine
- Amazon Parts Finder integration: requires fitment data in correct format
- No emissions defeat devices
- OBD devices: cannot circumvent EPA regulations
- Title format: Brand + Part Name + Part Number + Compatibility (Year Make Model)

## Industrial & Scientific
- **Partially gated**
- Hazardous materials: SDS required, hazmat fees apply
- Lab equipment: accuracy specs required
- Electrical components: voltage, wattage, amperage required
- OSHA compliance for safety equipment claims

## Fine Art
- **Gated**
- Original artwork: authenticity documentation
- Prints: must indicate limited edition and print run
- No unauthorized copies

## Dietary Supplements
- **Gated**
- FDA disclaimer required: "These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA..."
- Substantiated claims only
- Supplement Facts panel required on packaging and in images
- No disease claims
- Third-party testing: increasingly expected by Amazon
- GMP certification from manufacturer recommended

## Gated Category Approval Process

1. Log into Seller Central
2. Catalog → Add Products → search for item
3. Click "Show Limitations" → "Apply to sell"
4. Upload required documents (invoices, certifications, photos)
5. Some categories: auto-approved
6. Timeline: varies from immediate to 1–2 weeks
7. If rejected: contact different authorized distributor, reapply

**Common rejection reasons:**
- Handwritten or edited invoices
- Alibaba/unverified suppliers (Amazon requires authorized distributors)
- Missing compliance certifications
- Brand name mismatch
- Poor account health metrics
