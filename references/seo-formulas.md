# Amazon SEO — Title Formulas & Keyword Strategy by Category

## Universal Title Formula
`[Brand] + [Product Type] + [Primary Keyword Phrase] + [Key Differentiator] + [Size/Count/Variant]`

First 80 characters are most critical — they display on mobile and in search snippets.

---

## Category-Specific Title Templates

### Electronics / Tech Accessories
`[Brand] [Product Name] [Model Number] – [Key Spec], [Compatibility], [Color]`
Example: *Anker USB-C Charger 65W, GaN Fast Charging, Compatible with MacBook Pro/Air, iPhone 15, Galaxy S24*

### Home & Kitchen
`[Brand] [Product Type] – [Material/Feature], [Size/Capacity], [Color], [Pack Count]`
Example: *HydroMate Stainless Steel Water Bottle – Vacuum Insulated, 32oz, Leak-Proof, Navy Blue*

### Clothing & Apparel
`[Brand] [Men/Women/Kids] [Product Type] – [Material], [Style], [Size Range]`
Example: *Goodthreads Men's Slim-Fit Merino Wool Crewneck Sweater – Machine Washable, S–3XL*

### Health & Beauty / Supplements
`[Brand] [Product Name] [Count/Size] – [Key Claim], [Key Ingredient/Feature]`
Example: *Vital Proteins Collagen Peptides 20oz – Unflavored, Grass-Fed, Supports Skin & Joints*

### Toys & Games
`[Brand] [Product Name] – [Age Range], [Theme/Feature], [Pack Count]`
Example: *LEGO Technic Land Rover Defender 42110 – Ages 11+, 2573 Pieces, 1:8 Scale*

### Sports & Outdoors
`[Brand] [Product Type] – [Key Feature], [Weight/Size], [Sport/Activity], [Color]`
Example: *Osprey Atmos AG 65 Backpacking Pack – Anti-Gravity Suspension, Medium/Large, Black*

### Tools & Home Improvement
`[Brand] [Product Name] [Model] – [Power/Spec], [Key Feature], [Includes]`
Example: *DeWalt 20V MAX Cordless Drill DCD777C2 – Brushless, Compact, 2 Batteries Included*

### Automotive
`[Brand] [Part Name] [Part Number] – Fits [Year] [Make] [Model] [Trim]`
Example: *ACDelco 41-993 Spark Plug – Fits 2014–2020 Chevy Silverado 5.3L V8*

### Baby & Toddler
`[Brand] [Product Type] – [Safety Feature], [Age Range], [Material], [Count/Size]`
Example: *Pampers Swaddlers Newborn Diapers – Size 1 (8–14 lbs), 198 Count, Umbilical Cord Notch*

### Books / Media
`[Title]: [Subtitle] by [Author]` (Standard format, no SEO stuffing)

## Keyword Strategy by Position

### Title Keywords (Highest Weight)
- 1–2 primary keywords with highest search volume
- Must read naturally — not a string of keywords
- Brand name (unless unknown brand, then lead with product type)

### Bullet Point Keywords (Medium Weight)
- Secondary keywords woven naturally into benefit statements
- Long-tail phrases that wouldn't fit in title
- Synonyms for title keywords
- Use case keywords ("for camping," "for kids 3–5 years")

### Description Keywords (Lower Weight, Still Indexed)
- Conversational long-tail phrases
- Question-based keywords ("how to," "best for")
- Repeat top keywords from title/bullets naturally 1x each

### Backend Search Terms (Indexed, Invisible to Customers)
- Spelling variations and common misspellings
- Foreign language equivalents
- Abbreviations and acronyms
- Synonyms not in visible copy
- Related keywords that don't fit anywhere else
- Do NOT repeat: brand name, words already in title/bullets, competitor names, prohibited terms ("best," "cheap," "free")
- 250-byte HARD LIMIT — exceeding this = all backend terms ignored

## Keyword Research Workflow

1. **Seed keywords:** Start with what you'd search to find the product
2. **Reverse ASIN research:** Use Helium 10 Cerebro or Jungle Scout on top 3 competitors — find what keywords rank them
3. **Volume sort:** Focus on keywords with >500/month search volume for title; long-tail for backend
4. **Buyer intent filter:** Prioritize transactional keywords ("buy," "for," specific use cases) over informational
5. **Competition check:** Low-competition high-volume keywords are gold
6. **Seasonal consideration:** Factor in seasonal keywords during relevant months
7. **PPC validation:** Run Sponsored Products campaigns to discover converting keywords; move proven converters into organic listing

---

## CRO-Focused Keyword Principles

- Keywords that drove PPC conversions at low ACoS = highest value organic keywords
- Rank keywords by: Volume × Conversion Rate × Competition Inverse
- A keyword that gets 100 searches and converts 20% beats one with 10,000 searches and 0.5% conversion
- External traffic keywords (from Google/social) should align with Amazon keywords for synergy
